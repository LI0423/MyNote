安装bookkeeper
1.准备环境
安装好zookeeper
2.在bookeeper01上解压缩bookkeeper-server-4.3.0-bin.tar.gz并重命名为bookkeeper
3.配置环境变量BOOKEEPER_HOME、PATH
4.mkdir bk-data
mkdir bk-txn
并且设置权限为755
5.进入conf/bk_server.conf
配置文件如下：
bookiePort=3181
journalDirectory=/home/hadoop/bookkeeper/bk-txn (write ahead log目录)  
ledgerDirectories=/home/hadoop/bookkeeper/bk-data (data 目录)  
zkLedgersRootPath=/ledgers (zookeeper上的根结点)  
flushInterval=100 (数据刷新到磁盘的间隔)  
zkServers=bookkeeper01:2181,bookkeeper02:2181,bookkeeper03:2181  
serverTcpNoDelay=true (tcp参数，如果都是几十字节以下的小请求且tps很高，可以设为false)  
6.将配置好的bookeeper分发下去
7.启动服务
格式化：
在bookeeper01上运行：
bookkeeper shell metaformat
启动：
在三台机器上分别运行：
bookkeeper bookie 启动
8.检测启动是否成功
bookkeeper shell listbookies -readwrite -hostnames

安装hedwig
1、在bookeeper01上解压缩hedwig-server-4.3.0-bin.tar.gz并重命名为hedwig
2、配置环境变量HEDWIG_HOME、PATH
3、修改配置文件/conf/hw_server.conf
zk_host=bookeeper01:2181,bookeeper02:2181,bookeeper03:2181
zk_prefix=/hedwig
4、启动服务端
hedwig server
5、启动客户端
hedwig console
